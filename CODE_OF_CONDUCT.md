# Code of Conduct

Chronoframe discussions should stay practical, respectful, and focused on making the project safer and more useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent, especially around bug reports and support requests.
- Keep criticism specific to the work.
- Respect privacy. Do not pressure people to share personal photos, file paths, EXIF data, or logs.
- Help keep safety claims precise.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Dismissing data-loss or security concerns without investigation.
- Repeated off-topic comments after a maintainer asks to refocus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; lock discussions; close issues; or block participants when needed to keep the project healthy.

If you see a security issue, use the private reporting process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead of public comments.
